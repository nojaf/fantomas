    HashMultiMap<'Key, 'Value> Type

    Namespace: Internal.Utilities.Collections
    Assembly: Fantomas.FCS.dll
    Base Type: obj
    All Interfaces: IDictionary<'Key, 'Value> , ICollection<KeyValuePair<'Key, 'Value>> , IEnumerable<KeyValuePair<'Key, 'Value>> , IEnumerable

    Hash tables, by default based on F# structural "hash" and (=) functions. The table may map a single key to multiple bindings.
